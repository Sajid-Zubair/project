WELDING
Welding is a fabrication process that joins two or more materials, usually metals or thermoplastics, by applying heat, pressure, or both, to melt and fuse the materials together. The resulting bond is strong and permanent, creating a single, cohesive piece.Welding is mostly used as a fabrication and reparing process in the industries.

Some of the applications of welding include :

  • Fabrication of ships
  • Pressure vessels
  • Automobile bodies
  • Offshore platform
  • Welded pipes
